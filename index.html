About

LawBot is a pilot project in legal automation. Over the past 4 months we learned a lot about the possibilities of automizing standard legal tasks. We got a lot of press traction and social comments. Now we are taking the time to develop the best possible platform to deliver the socially desirable outcome we hope to achieve.

LawBot 2.0

In June, LawBot will relaunch as a Facebook messenger bot. LawBot is currently training on large data-sets to improve its understanding of user requests. We want to make LawBot a useful tool for people with legal problems. We think cutting-edge AI will help us get there.
